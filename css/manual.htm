CONTROLS:

WSAD/arrows = move camera

spacebar = pause to issue orders

number keys 1-9 = select character.  Double-press to go to character.

 +~ whatever key is left of your 1 key = select all characters

 F5 = quicksave

 F9 = quickload

 control key or middle mouse = rotate camera

Left click = select characters

Right click = issue orders / move characters

Hold Right click on target = bring up actions list on target (eg attack, talk, loot, heal)

Right click on character portrait = camera follow mode

Check the main website for more detailed instructions and tutorials.

 

NOTES ON SETTINGS

The game will run much faster in fullscreen mode

Turning up the population and squad size multipliers in the options will significantly slow performance.

Anti-alias won't work but you can turn it on in your nVidia control panel (especially for transparency, that will improve hair and grass a lot)

For more performance, reduce the foliage/grass, shadows and view distance settings

 

GETTING STARTED, WHAT CAN I DO?

  • You won't survive alone. Go into nearby bars (buildings with a sign that has a bottle on it) and talk to the guys named "sword for hire".  You can recruit these guys.
  • Click the "map" button to see a world map.  It has all the towns marked on it.
  • There are also shops in each town where you can buy weapons and armour.
  • You need a medkit in your inventory to heal wounds.  A good medic can often rescue your whole team after they lose a battle.
  • If you go to a trade center you can buy trade goods.  Find a cheap product and then go and sell it in another town for a profit.  You can make a lot of money this way.
  • The desert is dangerous, its full of bandits who are stronger than you.  Take your time, be cautious, build up your strength and join in other peoples battles until you are strong enough to hold your own.  Soon you will be kicking ass.

 

WHAT FEATURES WILL BE ADDED IN THE FUTURE?

  • Cannibals, slavers, police, and enemies that can capture and imprison your men, who you can rescue with your remaining guys.
  • Lots more factions and complexity to the world, improvements to the AI
  • Later on you will be able to use your owned buildings to open a shop, or a hospital.
  • Ability to join factions and do dynamic missions for them.  AI factions will also give missions to other factions and NPCs, not just you.
  • Building and town siege warfare.
  • The world map will be a lot bigger.  Towns will be procedurally generated, meaning I can fill the whole world single-handed and it will be different every time you start a new game.
  • Ranged combat.  Crossbows and harpoon turrets.
  • Unarmed combat
  • Thief skills, stealing, sneaking, escaping from jail.
  • Survival elements, you will need a supply of food and water, and need shelter to survive storms.  Endurance and fitness will factor in to combat.
  • Limb amputation, and robotic replacements
  • Blood sprays, splats, lots of blood
  • More animations to represent impact, injuries, damage and exhaustion.
  • Dialog system that will be linked into the AI.  Characters will say things at times based on their intentions, motivations, opinions and events.
  • More races are planned, other than just humans.
  • Wildlife, creatures, monsters, herds.
